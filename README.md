# Python Turtle Graphics Project

## Project Description:

This Python-based Turtle Graphics animation simulates a turtle going on a vacation with two destination choices: California or Washington. Depending on the chosen location, the turtle embarks on a unique journey:

California: The turtle explores a scenic mountainous area, builds a house and a Christmas tree, and relaxes in nature.
Washington: The turtle settles in a small town, builds a home, plants trees, and enjoys the sunny weather. It experiences a beautiful evening with a rainbow and ends the day gazing at the stars and the moon.

## Key Highlights

**Interactive Animation:** - The turtle moves through different locations, mimicking a vacation trip.
**House & Tree Construction:** - The turtle draws houses and trees in both locations using Turtle Graphics.
**Natural Exploration:** - The turtle enjoys natureâ€™s beauty, from trees to mountains and even a rainbow.
**Day-Night Cycle:** - The animation transitions from daytime activities to a peaceful night under the stars.

Technologies Used:

- **Control Structures**
